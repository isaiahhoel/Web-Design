About Me

Welcome to my Website! I have studied and worked hard to create this site so I hope you enjoy!

My full name is Isaiah Patrick Hoelscher and have grown up my whole life in Union, MO with my Mom and Dad. I Graduated from Union HS in 2020 and have since been at Hannibal-LaGrange University studying Computers and playing both track and baseball. I enjoy all sports and love to challenge myself to perfect each one. I also enjoy exploring the outdoors, traveling, and playing video games.

Family has been one of the biggest influences in my life, whether it was bad influences that would get me in trouble or good influences that would set me up for success later in life. Athletics/Academics

Click the Image to go to the Athletics Page

Growing up I have always been super competitive, whetherit was on the field or in the classroom, I have always been one to try and be the best at everything. In the classroom it was more about me being the first one done above being the most accurate on the test so as I progressed through school I had to learn that in order to be the fastest and the most acurate, I had to know as much as I could before taking the test or doing the homework. I have always loved baseball and soccer, other sports were fun and something extra to do but they were nothing compared to baseball and soccer until I finally tried track my senior year. Although I was unable to do it my senior year due to covid, I was given the opportunity to try it in college along with doing baseball and I have loved it, not becuase I was super good, but that it was hard to be good and only certain people could be good so it became a new challenge and pushes me daily to train and get better and better while still enjoying my love for baseball and keeping up with all of my studies.
