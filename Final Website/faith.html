CIY

CIY (Chirst In Youth) is a summer camp for High School students, it is full of energy, worship, and fantastic preaching no matter who they have speaking. This is the first place I truly took a leap towards my own faith, even though I had gone to church my whole life. It's where I made the decision to get baptized, where I made huge relationships with life long mentors and made many of my close friends I still have today.

Fall of 2021 was my first out of state mission trip I was able to be a part of. A group of 11 of us got together and made our way to Appalachia, KY over our fall break and we had the wonderful opportunity to serve a group called Club 180. They were a group that took in kids in the area that were either in poverty or had a weak backbone at home and gave them whatever they needed as well as teaching them the word of God. While we were there, we completely stripped an old nasty apartment of all its weaknesses and repainted it to look brand new for the families that would move in.
